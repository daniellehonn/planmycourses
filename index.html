📋 Setup Instructions

Step 1: Access the Template

  1. Open the Google Sheets template: Course Planner Template

    2. If you would love to help and contribute a template for your specific major at your specific school, please share your sheet or reach out to me at daniellehonnn@gmail.com!

  2. Click File → Make a copy to create your own editable version

Step 2: Customize Your Course List

Replace the sample data with your own courses

✨ PLEASE NOTE: If you have fulfilled a course elsewhere (eg. CC, AP, IB, etc.), please don't include it in your course list or list it as a prerequisite/corequisite!

For each course, fill in:

  1. Course Name & Number (e.g., "CS 101"):
  2. Taken:
    • Write Not Assigned if you haven't taken it yet
    • Otherwise, use format like: "Fall, Year 1"
  3. Prerequisites:
    • Write None or list course numbers separated by commas
    • ⚠️: These must exactly match the course names elsewhere in the spreadsheet
  4. Corequisites:
    • Write None or list course numbers separated by commas
    • ⚠️: These must exactly match the course names elsewhere in the spreadsheet
    • 📅: Corequisite courses will be automatically placed in the same quarter
  5. Units: How many units the course is worth
  6. Difficulty (1 = easiest, 8 = hardest)
    • Skip this if unsure — defaults to 1

✨ Pro Tip: Put your most important courses at the top — the planner uses this order to prioritize!

Step 3: Export Your Data

  1. In your Google Sheet, go to File → Share → Publish to web
  2. In the dropdown menu, change from "Web page" to "Tab-separated values"
  3. Click Publish and copy the generated link

Step 4: Connect to Course Planner

  1. Paste the copied link into the Load Course Data field

Important: Verify the link ends with output=tsv before submitting

You can mess around with some of the settings to get your desired results (eg. increase the max units per quarter, etc.)

🎉 Congrats! Your course planner is now ready to go!
